name: grill-with-docs
description: A relentless interview to sharpen a plan or design that also builds the project's shared language (CONTEXT.md) and records ADRs as you go. Use when grilling a change that introduces or sharpens domain terminology or hard-to-reverse decisions.
---

Run a `grilling` session, using the `domain-modeling` skill to maintain the project's shared language and record decisions inline.

As you grill:

- When a term is fuzzy or overloaded, sharpen it and capture it in `CONTEXT.md` (see `domain-modeling`).
- When a decision is hard to reverse, surprising without context, and the result of a real trade-off, offer an ADR via `documentation-and-adrs`.

Don't batch — capture terms and decisions the moment they crystallise, not at the end.
